Game Rules

  • Select the lock pin buttons in a sequnece to unlock the door.
  • You have 5 lock picks to unlock the door.
  • For every incorect sequence you will break a lock pick, So choose wisely for if you run out of picks its game over.
  • You will gain an additional lock pick for every door you unlock.
  • Once all pins are correct unlock the crypt and move onto the next level.
    • Good luck!
