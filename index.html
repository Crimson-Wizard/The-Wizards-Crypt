Welcome to the Wizards Crypt

Steal the wizards spellbook by unlocking the crypt doors. Select the pin buttons in a sequnece to unlock the door. You have 5 lock picks to unlock the door. Once all pins are correct unlock the crypt and move onto the next level. Good luck!
